## 💡 Pro Tips from Week 1

### What Worked:
✅ Providing value before asking
✅ Being genuinely helpful
✅ Responding to everyone
✅ Soft mention before hard ask
✅ Social proof ("Join 25 members")
✅ Personal messages to close circle
✅ Seeding group with content

### What Didn't Work:
❌ Asking for members on Day 1
❌ Generic "Join my group" posts
❌ Posting without engagement
❌ Expecting instant results

### Biggest Mistakes to Avoid:
1. **Asking too early** - Build trust first (3-4 days minimum)
2. **Being salesy** - Focus on value, not promotion
3. **Ignoring engagement** - Reply to every comment
4. **Giving up early** - Days 1-3 feel slow, keep going!
5. **Empty group** - Seed with content before inviting
